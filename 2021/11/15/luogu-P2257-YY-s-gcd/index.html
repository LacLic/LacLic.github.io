思路

莫比乌斯反演（虽然本质上是容斥，在这里推公式更好做，但有时候容斥更好想）

参考自JustinRochester的blog

$$
\begin{aligned}
(p \in &\ prime)\
Ans &=\sum p \sum{i=0}^N \sum_{i=0}^M [gcd(i,j)==p] \
&= \sum p \sum{i=0}^{\lfloor \frac{N}{p}\rfloor} \sum_{j=0}^{\lfloor \frac{M}{p}\rfloor} [gcd(i,j)=1] \
&= \sum _p \sum _{k=0} ^{min(\lfloor \frac{N}{p}\rfloor,\lfloor \frac{M}{p}\rfloor)} \mu(k) \cdot \lfloor \frac{\lfloor \frac{N}{p}\rfloor}{k}\rfloor \cdot \lfloor \frac{\lfloor \frac{M}{p}\rfloor}{k}\rfloor \
&= \sum _p \sum _{k=0} ^{min(\lfloor \frac{N}{p}\rfloor,\lfloor \frac{M}{p}\rfloor)} \mu(k) \cdot \lfloor \frac{N}{p\cdot k}\rfloor \cdot \lfloor \frac{M}{p\cdot k}\rfloor \
(&T=k\cdot p) \
&= \sum _p \sum _{T=0} ^{min(N,M)} [p|T]\cdot \mu(\frac{T}{p}) \cdot \lfloor \frac{N}{T}\rfloor \cdot \lfloor \frac{M}{T}\rfloor \
&=\sum _{T=0} ^{min(N,M)} \lfloor \frac{N}{T}\rfloor \cdot \lfloor \frac{M}{T}\rfloor \sum _p [p|T]\cdot \mu(\frac{T}{p}) \
&=\sum _{T=0} ^{min(N,M)} \lfloor \frac{N}{T}\rfloor \cdot \lfloor \frac{M}{T}\rfloor \sum _{p\in Prime,p|T} \mu(\frac{T}{p})
\end{aligned}
$$

考虑预处理，然后就能算，再数论分块就能达到

记，由于内质数个数约为7e5个，我们可以枚举质数在跑出每个质数对不同F(T)的贡献并叠加
